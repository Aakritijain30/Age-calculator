# Age Calculator

A clean, fast, free age calculator that tells you your exact age — down to years, months, and days — plus fun facts like total heartbeats lived, breaths taken, and how many days until your next birthday.

**Live demo → (https://aakritijain30.github.io/Age-calculator/)**

---

## What it does

- Exact age in years, months, and days
- Total months, weeks, days, and hours lived
- Fun stats — heartbeats, breaths, nights slept
- The weekday you were born on
- Next birthday countdown (days remaining)
- Optional: calculate age between any two custom dates

---

## Tech stack

Plain HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript — no frameworks, no dependencies, no build step. One file. Works everywhere.
